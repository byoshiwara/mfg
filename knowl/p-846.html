If \(\log_{3}{7}=x\), then \(3^x = 7\). We will use the graph of \(y = 3^x\) to approximate a solution to \(3^x = 7\). Graph \(Y_1 =3\)^ X and \(Y_2 = 7\) in the standard window (ZOOM 6) to obtain the graph shown in FigureÂ 1.55.
